Westfield Location

 

Designed to compliment our stand-out therapy programs, we created our Westfield center to be like no other. Individualization is at the very core of our Westfield center. We created a colorful, engaging environment designed to mimic a true community, while structuring learning opportunities at every stage of development. From the individually decorated houses, to the art that fills the walls, every inch of our facility was designed to promote the growth and happiness of the children we serve.

 

 

Avon Location

 

Our Avon center partners with the community, working together to provide the best possible experience for the children we serve. With a huge outdoor area, access to the YMCA pool, and a high-energy staff; our facility paired with our programs maximizes both skills and smiles. Taking it a step further, our partnership with HRH facilitates a comprehensive approach to reaching developmental milestones by offering speech, occupational therapy, and physical therapy all on site.

 

 

Broad Ripple

 

Our Broad Ripple location was designed with naturalistic teaching in mind. We created a vibrant environment that fosters maximum opportunities for learning while exploring, signing, dancing, building, and many other play activities. Located in the heart of the community, our children have ample opportunity to practice skills outside our center while still receiving 1:1 attention.
